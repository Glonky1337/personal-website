Welcome to my Website!

I'm Kevin Ferguson, a dedicated IT professional transitioning from a role as a Marine Corps IT Officer to a ServiceNow Application Developer. My passion for technology, combined with a strong foundation in business administration and a collection of IT certifications, has equipped me to take on the challenges of the ever-evolving tech landscape.

My journey began with a bachelor's degree in Business Administration from the University at Buffalo. From there, I ventured into the world of IT, earning certifications in ITIL4, CSM by ScrumAlliance, and Security+ by CompTIA. My most recent accomplishment is obtaining the CSA certification from ServiceNow, a milestone that marked my transition into the ServiceNow ecosystem.

During my time in the Marine Corps, I developed key skills in stress management, adaptability, and pushing beyond my comfort zone. These experiences, combined with my technical expertise, have prepared me to take on the role of a System Administrator and Developer in the ServiceNow ecosystem. Currently, I am participating in the ServiceNow NextGen Externship through the DoD Skillbridge program, an experience that has further solidified my passion for this platform.

As I prepare to transition out of the military in March 2024, I am enthusiastic about the opportunity to bring my unique blend of skills, dedication, and work ethic to new challenges in the ServiceNow community. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and drive success in your business operations.
